Fried Rice

  • Rice
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Green onion
  • Chili oil
  • Salt
  1. First beat eggs together in a bowl. Scramble the eggs in the wok and then remove.
  2. Cook chicken in wok until cooked through.
  3. Add in chili oil and green onion until fragrant.
  4. Add in cooked rice and eggs.
  5. Season with salt and enjoy.
