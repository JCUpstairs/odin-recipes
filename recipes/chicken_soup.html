Chicken Soup

  • Chicken breast
  • Water
  • Carrots
  • Potatos
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Dried dates
  1. Fill pot with cold water and put chicken in.
  2. Bring the water up to a boil and reduce to a simmer.
  3. Add in onion and ginger..
  4. After an hour has passed, put int he carrots and potatos.
  5. The soup is done once the chicken is tender and the carrots and potatos are soft.
