Shin Ramen

  • Shin Ramen packet
  • Eggs
  1. Fill pot with cold water and bring to a boil
  2. Add in dried noodles as well as seasoning and dried vegetables packets.
  3. Chop up the green onion and put it in.
  4. Crack two eggs into the pot. You may stir them into the soup if you prefer that over whole eggs.
